About

I'm an Information Technology student at Ateneo de Naga University who likes the whole arc of a product — from a rough idea, to the database schema underneath it, to the interface someone finally clicks on.

Most of my time goes into the web stack and into data work: writing the queries, shaping the pipelines, and making sure the numbers actually mean something by the time they reach a page. I care about code that's plain to read and about details that survive contact with real users.

Outside of coursework I build small projects to learn things properly, break them, and rebuild them. If you're working on something in that space, I'd like to hear about it.
